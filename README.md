<div align="center">
    <h1>LibreFlag.js</h1>
    <p>Feature flags that live in your app and your database. Open source. Ready in minutes.
</div>
<br />

LibreFlag.js is a feature flag library that aims to offer everything you would expect from a full feature flagging and feature management system, including incremental rollout, segmentation and feature lifecycle management, all without giving up control. Plug it into your backend, and bring your own database. No SaaS subscription or self-hosting infra needed.
